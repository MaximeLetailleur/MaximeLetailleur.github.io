PORTFOLIO TASKS - CC3 - DEBATING

UK growth in numbers

At the Festival of Economics, Russell Jones made a case for the "Half a Century of British Economic Decline"

UK Real GDP 1960-2020

This point is debattable, especially when confronted with the real evolution of the UK economy over that period. British GDP has risen steadily in the latter part of the 20th century and early 21st century

UK productivity

At the Festival, a panel was held to discuss sluggish UK productivity since the Great Recession. Is that an observable phenomenon?

UK productivity, 1975-2024

We can indeed observe a shift in the productivity trend after the 2008 financial crisis. Productivity grows at a lower rate during the 2010s, supporting the point made by the panelists

PORTFOLIO TASKS - CC8 - BIG DATA

Graph of the price evolution of frozen beef patties

Here is a graph charting the price evolution of beef patties in 1 british stores in 2023-24. The data from Davies & McElvoy (2024) was used. I joined the items and prices datasets using a common key, and narrowed it down to 1 store and 1 product. Grouping the many observations into daily, then monthly averages, I ended up with a handful of datapoints for this product that i could chart in a clear and readable way.

The Premier League has Become More Competitive

Since the 1990s, the EPL has become more competitive, with the League winners and runners-up accumulating more and more points.
